Short Company Profile

Marel Concepts International Limited is a company registered in the Federal Republic of Nigeria.

Marel Concepts International Limited is a construction, resource acquisition, and facilitating company. Marel works jointly with the Nigerian National Petroleum Company (NNPC) as Sellers' Facilitators. This venture is based on a concession with vendors being the concessionaire, with Marel being the selected and assigned operators.

Marel Concepts is the partner that provides negotiating platform for the oil and petroleum Sector and is therefore partnering with Buyers Facilitators and others in the petroleum sector.

We offer market for Bonny Light (BLCO), Aqua Lite, Forcados, Jet Fuel, etc. We provide trusted contacts with the Nigerian Petroleum Company, Ministry of Petroleum, owners of allocations, etc.

We also offer other services depending on Buyers requirements like TTO, TTT, CIF and FOB all around the Globe.

For our sale we only deal with direct mandates of serious buying companies or with direct contacts with the buying company.

We will share additional information, sale procedures, etc. on written request through Sell and Purchase Agreement (SPA), accompanied with Buyers Company Information.
